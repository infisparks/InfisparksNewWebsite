Our Journey

Our History

Infisparks was founded with a mission to deliver custom, high-quality software solutions that truly empower businesses and their people.

2018
Infisparks is Founded

We began our journey with a small team dedicated to creating custom web solutions for local businesses, focusing on elegant design and robust functionality.

2020
Expanded into Mobile Development

Recognizing the demand for mobile applications, we expanded our services to include Android and iOS app development, successfully launching our first major mobile project.

2022
Pioneered AI Integration

We introduced AI-integrated software services, helping clients leverage machine learning and artificial intelligence to create smarter, more efficient business applications.

2024
Launched 24/7 Client Support

To better serve our growing client base, we established a dedicated 24/7 support and live handling team, ensuring seamless operations for all our partners.
