Custom Over-the-Top (OTT) Streaming Platform Solutions

We design and engineer bespoke video and audio streaming services, giving you full control over content delivery, monetization, and user experience across all web, mobile, and smart TV devices globally.

Trusted by businesses in various industries
Multi-Device Delivery and Global Scalability
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Launch your service simultaneously on web, iOS (iPhone/iPad), Android, and Smart TV platforms from a single codebase, ensuring consistent quality.
  • Secure Content Delivery (CDN): Utilize advanced Content Delivery Networks to ensure low-latency, high-quality streaming for a global audience, regardless of user location.
  • Scalable Infrastructure: Build on a robust, cloud-based architecture designed to handle exponential growth in subscribers and concurrent viewers without degradation.
Flexible Monetization and User Retention Features

Implement diverse monetization strategies including Subscription Video On Demand (SVOD), Transactional Video On Demand (TVOD), and Ad-Supported Video On Demand (AVOD). We build personalized recommendation engines and user analytics dashboards to boost retention and maximize revenue.

Advanced Content Management and Security

Our Custom Content Management System (CMS) provides complete control over uploading, categorizing, and scheduling content. Furthermore, we integrate Digital Rights Management (DRM) and robust security measures to protect your premium video and audio assets from piracy and unauthorized access.
